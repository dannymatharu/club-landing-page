How the numbers are calculated

Proof of Play applies published proxy values from HM Treasury's WELLBY framework and Sport England's 2023/24 Social Value Model to your club's own data. Every source is named and every assumption is documented. The full methodology is publicly available.

Figures are evidence-informed estimates — not independently audited. Present them as such and pair with your own local qualitative evidence for the strongest impact.

📋

Funding applications

Sport England, the Football Foundation, Barclays, the National Lottery — they all want community impact evidence. Not a paragraph about what you do. Numbers. Your report gives you exactly that.

🏟️

Pitch lease conversations

Local authorities are cutting budgets. A club that can show £1.31M in community social value is having a different conversation to a club that says "we've been here forty years." Both can be true. Only one saves the lease.

🤝

Sponsors and partners

Businesses increasingly need to show social return on their community investment. A club with a social value ratio and health savings estimate gives a sponsor something to put in their ESG reporting. That changes the conversation.

📣

AGM and club communications

Tell your members, your committee, your volunteers what their club is actually worth. The collective effort of 157 volunteers contributing £764K in economic value is a different conversation to the usual AGM report.

Local media and PR

"Local club generates £1.31M in community value" is a story. A press release built around a credible number gets picked up. Generic club news doesn't.

NGB & governing body submissions

Accreditation, welfare submissions, NGB development programmes — your report gives you structured, evidence-backed data you can draw on across all of these, whatever sport you play.

How to access

Free through your County FA,
Active Partnership or NGB.

  • 1
    Contact your County FA, Active Partnership or NGB
    Proof of Play is licensed to the organisations that support your club — County FAs, Active Partnerships and National Governing Bodies. Your first step is asking yours if they have it. Football clubs go via their County FA. Cricket, rugby, tennis, athletics and all other sports go via their Active Partnership or NGB.
  • 2
    They share your access link
    If they're licensed, they'll send you a direct link to the tool. County FA and Active Partnership links are area-specific. NGB links are sport-specific — same tool, same report, just routed through your governing body.
  • 3
    Complete the assessment
    Participants by age and gender. Volunteers and their hours. Community programmes. Club infrastructure. Annual income if you want a social value ratio. About ten minutes.
  • 4
    Your report is ready immediately
    Full social value breakdown, social value ratio, press release, social posts and funding guide — all generated from your club's own numbers, ready to use straight away.
